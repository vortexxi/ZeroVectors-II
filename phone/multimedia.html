Augmented reality app

 

1. Download and unzip the app here. You'll find two files Alebrijes.apk and ZV_tracker.jpg.

2. Install Alebrijes.apk in your Android device.

3. Print the tracker image or open it in a screen.

4. Launch the app, your device camera will open, point it to the tracker image and voilá.

5. Press the buttons to change characters.
