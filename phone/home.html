About

 

Web design and multimedia.  Front-end solutions, from a button to a entire site with an artistic and beauty approach. Diverse economy, pay with money and other goods and services. Creativity in the web.

 

Our vision

 

Sites must be beauty, fast and accessible. We love beauty. Nature inspire us. Art, science and technology are our passion. We are a few creative curious weirdos looking for new alternatives to optimize and improve time, work and results, web craftsman. Zero Vectors is a small young project, so we supply a friendly, close and personalized attention. We also rent ourselves as freelancers if you are a big company.

We have activities in Europe and America, but we work online, so we can provide service anywhere.

What and how?

 

Any front-end resource for your site or the whole site: buttons, banners, menus, audio, 2d and 3d galleries, gifs and cinemagraphs, webGL content, virtual tours, augmented reality. Our tools: Adobe (Dreamweaver, Muse, Photoshop, After Effects, Illustrator, Premiere Pro), Logic Pro X, Reason 5, Autodesk (Maya, 123D Design), Unity 3D, Dialux, Three js, Vuforia SDK.

You are invited to explore our site so you can have a taste.

Coming soon: SEO.

 

We do not provide web hosting nor community management services.

Dealing

 

We believe and we are interested in developing a diverse economy, so depending on the client and the project, whenever possible, we are open to accept deals that include money and other goods and services that interest us as gadgets, software and hardware, pieces of art, musical instruments, professional gear, beer... we are open to proposals, lets make it interesting, lets make a deal!

 

Note: If you offer some good that in terms of the market is worth more than the service that we are providing, you accept the exchange without any refund, as Zero Vectors seeks to be a source of income rather than a source of debt.
